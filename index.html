Virtual Impactor Calibration Test Schematic (Spring 2022)

Created an experimental setup for virtual impactor flowrate, ratio, and cutoff size calibration. Pressurized air flow leads into a nebulizor, which creates a size distribution of aerosols between 0 and 10 microns diameter. Various instruments assist in diverting the flow into the virtual impactor, and the downstream is measured by an optical particle sizer.
