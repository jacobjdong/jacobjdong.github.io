FLAPPY PLANE

At every hackathon American Airlines sponsors, we love to give y'all a break from hacking to try your hand at making and throwing paper airplanes. Unfortunately, due to COVID-19 hackathons are virtual, so we needed to improvise.

So we made Flappy Plane...


HELPFUL HINTS

Here are some helpful hints when you're ready to get started:

  • You can use the space bar OR your mouse to jump
  • Avoid crashing into pipes to increase your score
  • The game ends when you clip a pipe with your plane OR if you hit the ground
  • If you find the game too easy/hard, change the difficulty!
